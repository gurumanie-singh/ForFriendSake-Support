1. Getting Started

1.1 Creating Your Account

To start using ForFriendSake:

  • Download the app from the iOS App Store
  • Sign up with your email address or phone number
  • Verify your account through the confirmation email or SMS
  • Set up your profile with a username and optional profile photo
  • Grant necessary permissions (location, notifications) for full functionality

1.2 System Requirements

  • iOS Version: iOS 18.0 or later
  • Device: iPhone or iPad
  • Storage: Minimum 100MB free space
  • Internet: Active internet connection required
  • Location Services: Required for check-ins and activity features

2. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create a squad?
Navigate to the Squads tab, tap the "+" button, enter your squad name and details, and invite friends via their username or email. You can customize squad settings including visibility and member permissions.
Q: How do I earn points?
Points are earned by checking in to activities, completing challenges, and participating in squad events. Each activity has a point value, and you must be within the designated location to successfully check in.
Q: Why can't I check in to an activity?
Ensure you're within the activity's location radius, have location services enabled, and the activity is currently active. Check-ins may be restricted by time windows set by the activity creator.
Q: Can I join multiple squads?
Yes! You can join multiple squads and participate in activities across all of them. Set a favorite squad in your profile settings for quick access.
Q: How do I leave a squad?
Go to the squad page, tap the settings icon, and select "Leave Squad." Note that leaving a squad will remove your access to squad activities and chat history.
Q: Are my location data and personal information secure?
Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures. Location data is only collected when you actively use location features. Review our Privacy Policy for detailed information.
Q: How do leaderboards work?
Leaderboards rank squad members based on points earned within specific time periods. There are weekly, monthly, and all-time leaderboards. Points reset at the beginning of each period.
Q: Can I change my username?
Yes, go to Settings > Profile > Edit Username. Note that usernames must be unique and follow our community guidelines.

3. Troubleshooting Common Issues

3.1 App Crashes or Won't Open

  • Force close the app and restart it
  • Ensure you have the latest version from the App Store
  • Restart your device
  • Check for iOS updates
  • Reinstall the app if the issue persists

3.2 Location Issues

Important: Location services must be enabled for check-ins and activity creation.

If you're having location issues:

  • Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services
  • Ensure Location Services are ON
  • Find ForFriendSake and set to "While Using the App" or "Always"
  • Enable "Precise Location" for accurate check-ins
  • Try toggling Airplane Mode on and off to refresh GPS

3.3 Push Notifications Not Working

  • Go to Settings > Notifications > ForFriendSake
  • Ensure "Allow Notifications" is enabled
  • Check notification settings within the app
  • Verify Do Not Disturb is not enabled
  • Log out and back in to refresh notification tokens

3.4 Login or Authentication Problems

  • Verify your email address or phone number is correct
  • Check for verification emails (including spam folder)
  • Use the "Forgot Password" option to reset your password
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Clear app cache or reinstall if issues persist

3.5 Data Not Loading or Syncing

  • Check your internet connection (WiFi or cellular data)
  • Pull down to refresh on list screens
  • Log out and back in to force a sync
  • Check if other apps can access the internet
  • Contact support if the issue continues

4. Feature Guides

4.1 Creating Activities

Steps to create an activity:
  1. Navigate to the Activities tab
  2. Tap the "Create Activity" button
  3. Enter activity name, description, and point value
  4. Set date, time, and duration
  5. Add a location (tap map to set precise location)
  6. Choose which squads can see and join
  7. Set check-in radius and time window
  8. Tap "Create" to publish

4.2 Managing Your Squad

Squad admins can:

  • Invite and remove members
  • Assign moderator roles
  • Customize squad settings and description
  • Create squad-specific activities
  • Manage chat permissions
  • View squad analytics and engagement

4.3 Using the Map View

  • Tap the Map tab to view nearby activities
  • Pins show active activities in your area
  • Tap a pin to view activity details
  • Use filters to show specific squad activities
  • Zoom and pan to explore different areas

5. Account Management

5.1 Updating Profile Information

To update your profile:

  • Go to Settings > Profile
  • Tap any field to edit (username, email, photo)
  • Upload a new profile photo from your camera or library
  • Save changes when complete

5.2 Privacy Settings

  • Control who can see your profile
  • Manage activity visibility
  • Block specific users
  • Adjust data sharing preferences

5.3 Deleting Your Account

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, including squads you created, activities, and chat history will be permanently deleted.

To delete your account:

  • Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account
  • Read the warning carefully
  • Enter your password to confirm
  • Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

6. Safety and Community Guidelines

6.1 Reporting Inappropriate Content

If you encounter inappropriate content or behavior:

  • Tap the three-dot menu on the content
  • Select "Report"
  • Choose the reason for reporting
  • Provide additional details if needed
  • Our team will review within 24-48 hours

6.2 Blocking Users

  • Go to the user's profile
  • Tap the three-dot menu
  • Select "Block User"
  • Blocked users cannot see your activities or contact you
